Frequently Asked Questions

How long can I keep my dumpster rental?

Rental periods are flexible, typically ranging from a few days to several weeks, depending on your project needs.

Are there weight limits for dumpsters?

Yes, each dumpster has a set weight limit. Our team will help you estimate the right capacity for your materials.

What can’t be placed in dumpsters?

Hazardous items such as paint, oil, tires, and chemicals are not allowed. We can suggest safe alternatives for disposal.

Do I need to be present for delivery?

Not necessarily. As long as the drop-off area is clear, our driver can place the dumpster as instructed.

Do you recycle materials?

Absolutely. We sort and send recyclable materials to certified facilities to minimize landfill impact.
